Story

My name is Andrew and I was born and raised in Los Angeles.

All throughout college (2020-2024), I took pictures of places I visited and events I attended.

In my senior year, while studying abroad in Mexico, I decided to take it seriously.

Since then, I started a photography Instagram account with over 20 posts to date.

From the very beginning, I didn't want to be just another photographer.

I wanted to create moments as much as capture them.
